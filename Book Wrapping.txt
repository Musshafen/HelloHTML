Book Wrapping
Start with all gift wrapping materials: wrapping paper, scissors, tape
Roll out enough wrapping paper to cover the book 
Place book upside down in center of paper
Bring paper up one side to cover book to make sure enough paper to cover whole book
Use scissors to cut straight line along the side of the attached Roll
Pull paper tautly up together covering the book in the paper
Place tape in center to to adhere both sides of the paper
Crease one end of the paper to fols over edge of book along the width of it
Fold down the two corners to create a triangle shape
Pull folded triangle shape down tight overtop Book
Place tape to adhere triangle shape to back of the paper
Repeat steps 9 through 12 until other side is done
